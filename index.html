Aim of

Dublin 7 Women’s Shed

Our aim is to provide a safe, welcoming space, a friendly environment where women from all walks of life can come together to impart information, knowledge, share skills and gain new skills. We strive to combat social exclusion amongst women in our community. We hope to try to breakdown any social and cultural barriers that women may face in their daily lives.
